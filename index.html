Did you know...

The people of Wakanda speak a real language.
Wakanda’s official language is a variant of Xhosa, which is primarily spoken in Zimbabwe and South Africa.

Michael Jackson once tried to buy Marvel Comics.
The reason? He wanted to star as Spider-Man in his version of the movie.

Originally, the Hulk had grey skin.
When the Hulk was first introduced in the comics, he was green-skinned due to a printing error with the four-color printing process.
